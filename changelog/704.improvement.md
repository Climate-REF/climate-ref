Adds CMIP7 support to the ozone diagnostics.
All five now build from the new `ref/recipe_ref_ozone_cmip7.yml` recipe.
The lat-time, SH October, NH March and annual-cycle diagnostics run on both CMIP6 and CMIP7, and the stratospheric zonal-mean profile stays on CMIP6 with its reference switched to the ESACCI-OZONE `L3-SAGE-OMPS` product.
