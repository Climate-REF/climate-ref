Ten ILAMB standard diagnostics now evaluate models against the obs4REF
reference datasets rather than the older bundled ILAMB reference files:
gross primary productivity (WECANN, FLUXNET2015), runoff (LORA),
soil carbon (HWSD-2-0), net biome productivity (Hoffman), snow cover (CCI-CryoClim),
burnt area (GFED-5-0), sea-surface temperature and salinity (WOA-23),
and the RAPID Atlantic overturning transport.
